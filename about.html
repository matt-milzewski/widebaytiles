Our Story

Serving the Fraser Coast for over 20 years

Our Journey

Founded in 2003, Wide Bay Tile Centre began as a small family business with a passion for quality tiles and exceptional service. Over the years, we've grown to become the Fraser Coast's premier destination for all things tiling.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with homeowners, builders, and designers throughout the region.
