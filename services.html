Our Services

At Wide Bay Tile Centre, we offer a comprehensive range of services to support your tiling project from start to finish.

Product Advice

Our experienced team provides expert advice on tile selection, layout, and design to help you achieve your vision.

Trade Pricing

Special pricing for trade professionals and builders. Register your business to access exclusive discounts.

Tile Cutting

Professional tile cutting service for precise results. We can cut tiles to your exact specifications.

Tool Hire

Rent professional tiling tools and equipment for your project. Quality tools for quality results.

Tiler Referral Network

After-sales Support

Our Process

1. Choose Your Tiles

Visit our showroom to explore our extensive range of tiles. Our experts will help you find the perfect match for your project.

2. Get a Quote

We'll provide a detailed quote based on your requirements, including materials and any additional services needed.
